About Cheragh:

“Cheragh” (It means Light in Persian) is a startup at early stages that connects candidates who are looking for a job with the opportunities that suit them best. It also offers a wide variety of educational contents and personality tests.
Cheragh is a job board used by employers to advertise their job vacancies to job seekers. Job seekers can use Cheragh to search for new job opportunities in their area and profession. They can build their resume online very easily on Cheragh and apply for job opportunities.
Cheragh includes all job categories and fields. Cheragh opportunities are update every day and job seekers with various levels of skills can find their right fit job opportunities and apply for them.
Cheragh also has Online Learning courses based on VOD, job seekers can participate in this courses and improve their abilities to be more skillful to increase their chance for getting better job opportunities.
