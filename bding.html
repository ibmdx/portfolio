1. About BDING



BDING provides proximity solutions for Iranian people and businesses. their solutions are based on ibeacon technology for now. they can bring context of every business on people's smart devices.
-

BDING's beacon technology is one of these exciting projects.
Using the new technology of low energy Bluetooth, beacons can make our lives easier and our desires more approachable.
Given the unique nature of this infant technology, by studying the global market and current beacon designs, we have come up with an initial design proposal for this local company which not only is beautiful and makes this product to be more insight, it can also complete with its global rivals and kick-start a new and major market of quality "IoT" and tech services.

We have implemented the latest design trends and low-cost manufacturing process with ease of use in mind.
A serious, modern and cutting-edge face which represents the forward-looking business of this emerging tech brand.
